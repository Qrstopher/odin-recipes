Meatloaf

Description

Easy and quick meatloaf recipe for the family for any occasion

Serves 8 Calories 640 kcal

Ingredients

  • ½ medium onion diced
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 2 eggs
  • ¾ cup milk
  • ¾ cup Italian breadcrumbs or seasoned breadcrumbs
  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup or chili sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley chopped
  • salt & pepper to taste
  • ½ cup chili sauce
  • ½ cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar optional

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Spray a foil lined baking tray with cooking spray and set aside.
  2. In a small pan, cook onions in butter over medium low heat until tender. Cool completely.
  3. In a medium bowl combine eggs, milk, and breadcrumbs. Allow mixture to sit for 5-10 minutes.
  4. Add the ground beef, onions, 1 tablespoon of ketchup or chili sauce, Italian seasoning, parsley and salt & pepper to taste. Mix until just combined.
  5. Form a 8"x4" loaf on the prepared baking tray. Bake for 40 minutes.
  6. Meanwhile, combine the chili sauce and ketchup (and brown sugar if using). Spread mixture over the meatloaf and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes or until cooked through and the meatloaf reaches 160°F. Broil 1-2 mintues if desired.
  7. Allow meatloaf to rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
